Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how this website collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information from visitors. This policy applies to the website and all information collected through or submitted to this platform. We are committed to transparency regarding data practices and respecting visitor privacy rights under applicable United States federal and state laws.

Scope and Application

This policy covers personal information collected through this informational website. The site serves as an educational resource about household pest management and does not provide commercial services or transactions. Information collection is limited to what visitors voluntarily provide through contact forms and standard web server logging.

Information We Collect

We collect several categories of information from website visitors:

Voluntarily Provided Information

When visitors use the contact form, they provide their name, email address, and message content. This information is collected only when visitors actively submit the form and is used solely to respond to their inquiries about website content or technical issues.

Automatically Collected Information

Our web server automatically logs standard technical information including IP addresses, browser types, device information, operating systems, referring pages, pages visited, time stamps, and geographic location data derived from IP addresses. This logging occurs through normal web server operations and helps us maintain site functionality and security.

Quiz Responses

Information entered into the pest identification quiz on our website is processed locally in the visitor's browser and is not transmitted to our servers or stored by us. Quiz responses remain on the visitor's device only.

Sources of Information

Information comes directly from visitors when they submit contact forms or indirectly through automatic web server logging during site visits. We do not obtain information from third-party sources, data brokers, or other external parties.

How We Use Information

Information collected serves specific purposes:

Contact form submissions allow us to respond to visitor inquiries about website content, address technical issues, and maintain communication regarding the informational resource. Server logs help us understand site usage patterns, identify technical problems, maintain security, prevent unauthorized access, and improve website functionality and content organization.

We analyze aggregate visitor data to understand which content areas receive attention and to identify potential improvements to the informational resource. All analysis uses anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify individual visitors.

Retention of Information

Contact form submissions are retained for the period necessary to respond to inquiries and maintain communication records, typically not exceeding two years unless ongoing correspondence necessitates longer retention. Server logs are maintained for security and operational purposes, with routine deletion of logs older than ninety days unless specific security incidents require extended retention for investigation.

Visitors may request deletion of their contact form submissions by contacting us at the email address provided above. Such requests will be honored within thirty days unless legal obligations require retention.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer personal information to outside parties. Information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers who assist with website hosting and technical maintenance may have access to server logs and contact submissions in the course of providing services. These providers are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality and use information only for providing their services.

Information may be disclosed if required by law, court order, or other legal process, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect rights and property, prevent fraud or abuse, or respond to emergency situations involving safety.

No Sale or Targeted Advertising

We do not sell personal information to third parties, nor do we share information for monetary or other valuable consideration. This website does not participate in targeted advertising, behavioral advertising, or cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not build visitor profiles for advertising purposes or share information with advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

This website does not knowingly collect information from children under thirteen years of age. The site is designed as a general informational resource for adults. If we become aware that information from a child under thirteen has been collected without verified parental consent, we will delete that information promptly. Parents who believe their child has provided information to this site may contact us using the information above.

Data Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include secure server configurations, restricted access to data, regular security assessments, and encrypted transmission of contact form data using SSL technology.

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Visitors use this website at their own discretion.

Visitor Rights and Choices

Under applicable federal and state privacy laws, visitors may have certain rights regarding their personal information:

Visitors may request access to personal information we have collected about them. Visitors may request correction of inaccurate personal information. Visitors may request deletion of their personal information, subject to certain exceptions for legal compliance and operational necessity. Visitors may opt out of future communications by contacting us.

To exercise these rights, visitors should contact us using the email address provided above. We will respond to verified requests within the time frames required by applicable law, typically within thirty to forty-five days.

Notice at Collection for California Residents

For residents of California, we provide the following additional notice pursuant to the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act:

Categories of personal information collected include identifiers such as names, email addresses, and IP addresses, internet activity information including browsing behavior and site interaction, and geolocation data derived from IP addresses. Information is collected directly from you when you submit contact forms and automatically through server logs during website visits.

We use this information for responding to inquiries, maintaining website operations, analyzing site usage, improving content and functionality, and maintaining security. We do not sell your personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

California residents have rights to know what information is collected, request deletion of information, correct inaccurate information, and opt out of any sale or sharing of information. The website honors Global Privacy Control signals where technically feasible.

Other State Privacy Rights

Residents of states with comprehensive privacy laws including Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and others have similar rights to access, correct, and delete personal information. These rights may be exercised by contacting us as described above. We process requests in accordance with applicable state law requirements.

Material changes will be indicated prominently on the website.

Compliance with Laws
